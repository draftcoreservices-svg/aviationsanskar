About

Why FlyTeach exists

Early‑career recruiting is stressful because universities don’t teach the real rules of how recruiters and interviewers judge you. FlyTeach exists to bridge that gap with practical coaching.

What this is

1‑to‑1 career and interview coaching for aviation & aerospace students, graduates, and early‑career engineers. It’s not generic careers advice or textbook prep — everything is based on industry experience in avionics systems integration, mission‑planning systems, and EASA‑style regulatory environments.

Recruiter mindset System‑level questioning Practical delivery

Who it’s for

  • Aviation/aerospace students nearing graduation
  • Graduates applying for first engineering roles
  • Early‑career engineers (roughly 0–3 years)
  • Graduate schemes, junior roles, defence‑adjacent careers

Who it’s not for

  • Academic tutoring
  • Chartered Engineer mentoring
  • Senior‑level career coaching
  • “Guaranteed outcomes” services

What you’ll get

Clarity, structure, and a realistic plan.

Stronger explanations

Turn uni projects into stories that make sense to engineers and recruiters.

Better interview structure

Answer frameworks for when you’re unsure — without sounding scripted.

Confidence under pressure

Practise the delivery so you feel calm and less robotic.
